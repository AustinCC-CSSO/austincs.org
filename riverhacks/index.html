About The Hackathon

River Hacks will be Austin Community College's first ever hackathon, open to all ACC students interested in building technology. You'll have the opportunity to create a product, learn new skills, and have fun along the way. The hybrid 24-hour hackathon will bring together students with the goal of creating a project under the theme of "INSERT THEME HERE". Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned hacker, we'll provide you with the guidance and all necessary tools to assist you along the way!
